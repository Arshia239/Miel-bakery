Our Story

A little bakery with a lot of heart.

Miel started in 2015 from a tiny kitchen in Karachi one oven, one baker, and a dream to bring European-style artisan baking to Pakistan. Today we bake over 500 items daily, but every piece still gets the same love as that very first croissant.
